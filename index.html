Grammar: Polite Requests

How do we ask for things politely at a cafe or restaurant?

"I want a coffee." (A bit rude!)

"I would like a coffee." (Polite!)

Fast English: "I'd like a coffee."

Select the best option for ordering:

RULE UNLOCKED!

Always use I'd like instead of "I want" to sound polite and native.

Tell the teacher politely: Ask for a slice of cake. "I'd like..."
